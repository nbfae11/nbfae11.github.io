Services for non-resident Indians

Just because you no longer live in India, it doesn't mean you can’t bank there. NBF has partnered with leading Indian private sector bank ICICI so you can maintain an account back home while living in the UAE.

NBF and ICICI Bank Limited offer a suite of financial solutions just for you, including savings accounts, fixed deposit account, home loan and an online trading account.

Enjoy these and other benefits when you open an NBF and ICICI Bank NRI account. It is all you will ever need for banking convenience and flexibility.

Product Features

  • Free online money transfers
  • 24/7 account access to your funds in India
  • Competitive interest rates on NRE Savings Account, NRO Savings Account and FCNR Fixed Deposit Account
  • Attractive interest rates on home loan
  • Completely online and real time trading through ICICIDirect.com
  • Online bill payment facility
  • Bank 24/7 through a widespread network of over 4,850 branches and 13,792 ATMs in India
  • A 24-hour contact centre and secure online banking

Other features & benefits

  • Tax-free interest due to the Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA)
  • Easy movement of funds: fully repatriable annual interest earned after tax, allowing you to take up to USD 1 million per year out of the country
  • Seamless re-designation of your resident account to an ICICI Non-Resident Ordinary Account when you become a NRI
  • Joint holding of savings accounts with an Indian resident or NRI
  • Access to exclusive offers in India and abroad
  • A free PO Box service to send your documents
