Priority Cards

Cards and rewards that match your status

For those who seek exclusivity, our premium credit and debit cards are complimentary to Priority Banking customers and feature unmatched benefits and lifestyle-enhancing rewards.

Products

  • Visa Infinite Credit Card

    NBF Visa Infinite, our most prestigious credit card, helps you enjoy the best life has to offer with a personal concierge who will manage your travel and leisure bookings wherever you may be. You will also get access to over 500 airport lounges, including Dubai international, as well as receive up to 3% cashback on all retail purchases.

  • Visa Platinum Debit Card

    Our top tier debit card, NBF Visa Platinum, lets you shop, travel and dine in style wherever you are. The card offers purchase protection on any lost, stolen or damaged goods, plus double the warranty on every item you buy. NBF Visa Platinum is accepted at over 1.4 million ATM locations and over 28 million outlets worldwide.

Eligibility

Join the Priority club today

You are eligible for NBF Priority status if you meet and maintain* one or more of the following criteria:

• You earn a monthly salary of AED 40,000 or more that is credited into an NBF account
• You maintain a minimum banking average balance of AED 350,000 (or the equivalent in any other currency) in deposits and/or investments
• Your mortgage value is over AED 2.5 million

*To avoid any inconvenience, you must maintain your Priority banking account with adequate funds that meet the eligibility criteria listed above. In the unfortunate event that your account fails to meet at least one of the criteria, your Priority Banking status will be changed to a relevant package in Personal Banking.
