Finance your Manchester Business School executive MBA with NBF

Valid Until 31 December 2018

Manchester Business School, Middle East Centre, Office F116, Block 2B, Dubai Knowledge Village

Are you looking to challenge yourself, change direction or take your career to the next level? National Bank of Fujairah now offers students of Manchester Business School’s MBA programme in Dubai exclusive funding options, so you can better focus on your studies while we take care of your finances.

Education Loan

  • Competitive interest rates
  • Loan amounts of up to 20 times your salary with tenor of up to 48 months
  • Simple application process and quick approvals

NBF Visa Platinum Exclusive Credit Card

  • Invest in your future with a free-for-life NBF Visa Platinum Exclusive Credit Card
  • Get peace of mind and pay your semester fee every month with NBF's Easy Payment Plans starting at 0% and no processing fee 
  • Up to 2% cashback rewards and complimentary access to over 600 airport lounges 
  • Work hard, play hard with 60% off movie tickets at VOX cinemas across the UAE 
  • We know studying can be expensive so enjoy a whole host of discounts on us including dining, accommodation and leisure options

Other key benefits 

  • Zero balance salary transfer account
  • No minimum balance required
  • No monthly maintenance fees
  • Free ATM withdrawals at any bank across the UAE
  • On-the-go access to your account through NBF Direct online banking and NBF Direct App

Home Loan 

  • Financing up to AED 20 million and 80% of the property value with a Home Loan
  • Competitive interest rates starting from 3.49% p.a. 

Auto Loan 

  • Financing of new and pre-owned cars with an Auto Loan
  • Tenor of up to five years with interest rates starting from 2.75% 

Islamic Banking 

  • Shariah-compliant Islamic products are also available
