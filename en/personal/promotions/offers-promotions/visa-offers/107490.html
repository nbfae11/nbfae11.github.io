Get 15% off on cosmetics and perfumery with Visa Platinum

Valid Until 31 December 2018

15% discount for Visa Platinum cardholders

15% discount for Visa Platinum cardholders

Kravt chain shops of perfumery and cosmetics occupy a leading position in the market of the Republic of Belarus: 12 stores in Minsk and 10 stores around the country. There is a wide range of decorative cosmetics, perfumes, body and face skin-care of all price range: from mass brands to selective luxury brands at Kravt stores.

The system of self-service is organized at all chain stores, which allows you to choose and try any product before purchasing. You can use the services of professional consultants, who are trained continuously in domestic and international training courses, which are held by manufacturers of luxury perfumes and cosmetics.

The permanent system of discounts is available for clients of the network, network events, promotion of brands, competitions and consultation with invited experts, cosmetologists and beauticians are held at chain stores.
