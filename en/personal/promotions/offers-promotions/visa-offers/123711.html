1. Eligibility
1.1 The Promotion is open to all principal and supplementary holders of Visa Credit, Debit and Prepaid cards issued in Philippines and abroad (“Cards”) (“Cardholders”).

2. Promotion Mechanics and Conditions
2.1 The Pre-Sale promotion commences on May 8 - June 15, 2018 (“Promotion Period”). All transactions must be made within the Promotion Period to avail of the Promotion.
2.2 This promotion covers Mamma Mia- the musical on September 29 - October 21, 2018 at The Theatre, Solaire Resort and Casino.
2.3 Tickets must be purchased using a Visa Credit, Debit or Prepaid card.
2.4 To avail of this Promotion, eligible Cardholders may purchase their Mamma Mia tickets at the following venues:
(a) All TicketWorld Outlets
(b) Online via www.ticketworld.com.ph. Use Promo Code: VISA
