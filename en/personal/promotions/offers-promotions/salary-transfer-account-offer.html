Open an NBF Salary Transfer Account and get all the premium perks for less

Valid Until 30 September 2018

Whether it’s travel, family time or life-changing experiences, do more of what you love for less with exclusive rewards and savings from NBF Preferred Banking.

Open an NBF Salary Transfer Account before 30 September 2018 and become an NBF Preferred Banking customer with access to all these benefits:

  • Free NBF Traveller International membership
    Discover loads of incredible offers at over 1,500 dining, entertainment and lifestyle hot spots with complimentary access to the NBF Traveller International app.
  • Competitive interest rates
    Make your money work as hard as you do with a current account that earns between 0.25% to 1.25% interest* every month.
  • Free money transfers
    Support your family and friends abroad with our easy online remittances, and you’ll get one free international transfer every month.
  • Free ATM withdrawals
    Experience the convenience of free cash withdrawals and balance enquiries from any of the 6,000+ ATMs in the UAE.
  • 60% off movie tickets
    Catch all the latest blockbusters with 60% cashback on movie tickets at select cinemas. Simply pay with your NBF credit card. 

Exotic holiday? Dream wedding? Apply for an NBF Personal Loan along with your NBF Salary Transfer Account and you’ll also get: 

  • Preferential rates
    A 1% reduction on the standard loan/finance rate, putting money back in your pocket.
  • Payment holiday
    A two-month payment holiday before the first instalment, giving you some breathing room to focus on what really matters.

Terms and conditions apply:

  • * Interest is only paid on balances above AED 10,000
  • Offer valid until 30 September 2018
  • Offer only available for salary transfers above AED 15,000
