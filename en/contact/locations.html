Notice to Customers

National Bank of Fujairah wishes to inform its customers that during the Holy month of Ramadan, banking hours are from 09:00am – 01:00pm, Saturday to Thursday. 

Fujairah City Centre Branch will be open according to the timings below.

  • Morning shift: 10:00am – 2:00pm
  • Evening shift: 9:30pm – 11:30pm

Al Reef EBSU timings are as follows: 

  • Morning shift: 10:00am – 4:00pm
  • Evening shift: 7:30pm – 12:30am

More ways to bank with us

You can continue to do your everyday banking at any NBF branch, but there are also other ways to bank with us that you may find more convenient:

  • 24/7 banking via NBF Direct online banking
  • Download the NBF Direct App 24-hour
  • Call Centre: 8008NBFUAE(543) (within UAE)
