Large Corporates & Government

Partnering with you for greater success

At National Bank of Fujairah, we are privileged to maintain longstanding, strategic partnerships with the business elite in the UAE including government-owned commercial entities, prominent family-owned business groups and a wide range of top tier corporates with turnovers exceeding AED 1 billion.

Our dedicated large corporate relationship management team consists of business advisors and technical experts capable of delivering timely, specialised, needs-based solutions that cater to your dynamic requirements.

We Provide:

Our specialised suite of product and advisory services include:

  • Syndicated and club deals
  • Commodity trade
  • Mezzanine debts
  • Forex and commodity hedging
  • Cash management
  • Factoring
