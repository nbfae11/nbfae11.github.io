NBF Elham

Specialised banking for Emirati businesswomen

In the UAE, women play an increasingly vital role in society and business and Emirati businesswomen are looking for a reliable banking partner who will support them on every step of their journey to success.

As a bank possessing an unrivalled understanding of customer needs and a firm commitment to the local economy, we are pleased to introduce NBF Elham, a specialised corporate banking unit dedicated exclusively to providing bespoke banking solutions to Emirati businesswomen.

As the first bank in the UAE to maintain such a dedicated focus on partnering with Emirati businesswomen, Elham leverages the bank's strong track record in supporting businesses in the region to take your ambitions to the next level.

Armed with local insight gleaned from more than 30 years of experience, our Elham team of highly skilled female Emirati relationship managers will support you as you continue to break new boundaries.

We Provide:

  • Business current account with no minimum balance requirement
  • Attractive rates on loan facilities
  • Preferential rates for local and international transactions
  • Cash management services
  • Trade services
  • Treasury services
  • Advisory services
  • Access to NBF's UAE branch network

Value Added Services:

  • Free business credit card, debit card and cheque book
  • Free online banking services
  • Minimal documentation requirements and fast approval times
  • 24-hour toll-free customer call centre

Contact us to discuss your requirements today.
